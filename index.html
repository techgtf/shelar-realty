About Project

Where Hills Whisper, Luxury Beckons, and Your Bungalow Awaits!

Surrounded by scenic hills and peaceful greenery, Mahalaxmi 16 Hill Winds by Shelar Realty offers the perfect mix of nature’s calm and city convenience. With a strong legacy across Undri, Handewadi, Wanowrie, and Wadki, this gated community is thoughtfully planned for those who value space, comfort, and luxury. Reserved for customizable villas and bungalows, it gives you the freedom to build a home that’s truly yours. With features like private boundary walls, 21-ft concrete roads, CCTV security, street lighting, well-planned drainage system, and lush landscaping, every detail ensures a lifestyle that’s secure and serene.
